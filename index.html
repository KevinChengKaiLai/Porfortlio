Co-first author, Unraveling the Alternative Process Configurations for More Environmentally Friendly Maleic Anhydride Production

Ting-Yu Yu, Ching-Tai Kuo, Cheng-Kai Lai ,Wangyun Won, Bor-Yih Yu

Process System Engineering Laboratory, National Taiwan University

This study proposes and evaluates alternative configurations for producing maleic anhydride (MA) through n-butane oxidation to enhance environmental friendliness. Using advanced numerical modeling with NumPy/SciPy, we achieved 97.52% energy efficiency and 99.9% purity. Our optimized configuration demonstrates superior environmental and economic performance compared to existing literature, with significant potential for industrial implementation.
